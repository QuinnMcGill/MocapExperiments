MOTION CAPTURE TESTCASES:
Here are the 4 testcases that I chose to use to evaluate different motion capture systems.
Each testcase is a video of different poses, performed by a single actor. Each pose should 
be held for 5 seconds. Every video should start with a neutral face pose.

# ---- Test 1: Emotion Classes ---- #
    1. Happiness (teeth showing)
    2. Sadness
    3. Fear
    4. Disgust
    5. Anger
    6. Surprise
    7. Contempt


# ---- Test 2: Eye Movements ---- #
    1. Right eye closed
    2. Left eye closed
    3. Both eyes closed, brows neutral
    4. Both eyes closed, brows up
    5. Both eyes closed, brows scrunched
    6. Left brow lifted, right brow lowered
    7. Right brow lifted, left brow lowered (omitted bc I can't do that)
    8. Eyes open, both brows lifted 
    9. Eyes open, both brows scrunched


# ---- Test 3: Mouth Movements ---- #
    1. Closed mouth smile
    2. Open mouth smile
    3. Lips puckered
    4. Right lip pull
    5. Left lip pull
    6. 'O' mouth pose
    7. Tongue out, mouth closed


# ---- Test 4: Weird, Complex Poses ---- #
    1. Cheeks puffed, brows up
    2. Cheeks sucked in, pursed lips
    3. Bottom lip bite
    4. Mouth open, pulled to right
    5. Mouth open, pulled to left
